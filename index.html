Designs

The Teacher

A 4-color screen print depicting Marcia Howard standing in George Floyd Square with the Fist Statue and the building containing Cup Foods in the background.

The Guy

An "exquisite corpse" relief print with (from top to bottom) a person made from circuit board traces meant to represent a sentient AI, just your average bog monster, and pixel art hands and legs walking. Exquisite corpses are an artistic exercise where you combine different themes and/or styles into one represented person. Here I have combined my love of technology (twice c;) and seasonally relevant themes. (I made this leading up to Halloween)

Circumscription

A three color relief print using different (dark) shades of grey. I used geometry to circumscribe (really! Circumscription doesn't necessarily have to involve circles!) different sizes of the three tessellatable regular polygons in different colors. This is a block reduction print.

Skeleton
